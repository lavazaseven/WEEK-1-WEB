Plant a tree for your birthday

Transform your birthday into a gift for the planet. For every birthday celebration, we plant a native or fruit tree in Kenya

About Birthdaytree Initiative Kenya

Our Story

Founded in 2023, birthdaytree initiative kenya began with a single idea: Instead of traditional birthday gifts, why not plant a tree seedling? What started as a small personal project by our founder MR Reynold Ajega Lavaza in his rural home in vihiga county,Kenya has grown into a national wide movement.

Why Trees For Birthdays?

Trees symbolise life and growth - perfect for birthdays! Each tree planted represents;

  • A personal connection to nature
  • A meaningfull environmental contribution
  • A legacy that grows year after year
  • A celebration that gives back to the environment

Each birthday tree planted;

  • Clean air for people annualy
  • prevents soil erossion
  • Is a natural habitate for local wildlife
  • Is a water cycle regulatore

Our Mission And Vision

Mission Statement

We aim to pravticaly conserve the environment by planting native trees for every birthday celebration, we aim to plant one million trees by 2050.

Our Vision

A kenya where every birthday contributes to aforestation, creating a nationalwide network of birthday forests tha combat climate change while honering personal celebrations
